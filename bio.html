As a first-year college student, my skill set is still expanding; however, I already have some experience with Java, HTML, CSS, Xcode, and Bootstrap. You can check out some of my work in my Sample Works tab.

As of now, I am unsure of where I want to go with my career because there are so many options, but my goal for the next four years is to create my own app, work in internships, and find a job where I can pursue my interests after I graduate from Georgia Tech.
