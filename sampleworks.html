A Collection of My Projects

MapView for UWTicketBuddy

UWTicketBuddy is a project started at HackGT 2016 by me, Brian Glowniak, Daniel Hathcock, and Yu-Lin Yang. It is an app that is meant to streamline the process of buying and selling football tickets at the University of Wisconsin - Madison because football is such a big deal there. I worked on the implementation of the Google Maps API and location services, which will help buyers and sellers see who is near them.
